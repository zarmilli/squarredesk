Our Policy

01

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of your appointment.

02

Cancellations made less than 48 hours before your appointment will forfeit the deposit. Rescheduling is available with 48+ hours notice.

03

Please arrive with a clean, moisturised face and no makeup. Arriving more than 15 minutes late may result in a shortened appointment.

04

Please disclose any skin conditions, allergies, or sensitivities prior to your appointment. Your skin health and safety are always the priority.
