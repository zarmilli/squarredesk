Booking Policy

01

A 30% non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date. This is applied toward the total package fee and confirms your booking.

02

The remaining balance is due 7 days before your session or event date. Late payment may result in cancellation of the booking.

03

Cancellations with less than 14 days' notice forfeit the retainer. Rescheduling is available once at no charge with sufficient notice.

04

All edited images are delivered via a private online gallery within 14 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
