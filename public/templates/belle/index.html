Our Policies

01

All custom orders require at least 5 days' notice. For wedding and tiered cakes, please order at least 3 weeks in advance.

02

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your order. The remaining balance is due upon collection or delivery.

03

Our products may contain or be prepared in an environment with nuts, dairy, eggs, and gluten. Please inform us of any allergies.

04

Delivery is available within a 20km radius. Please contact us for a delivery quote before placing your order.
